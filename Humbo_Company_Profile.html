ABOUT US

Intelligence Begins at the Sensor

Founded in April 2026, Humbo Technology is an independent design house specializing in BLE smart sensing solutions. We deliver two product lines: the SH-100/SH-200 BLE Sensor Hub — a multi-channel modular sensing platform with Edge AI processing — and the HB100/HB120 SmartBeacon — an ultra-low-cost, no-code BLE beacon for disposable smart label applications. We serve clients in cold chain logistics, smart manufacturing, smart buildings, healthcare, and smart city segments across Taiwan and Japan.

CORE TECHNOLOGY

Integrated Sense · Compute · Transmit

Our BLE IC platform integrates sensing, edge processing, and wireless transmission into a single chip — dramatically reducing system complexity, power consumption, and time-to-market.

S
Multi-Channel Sensing
SENSING
Drives up to 8 flexible sensor channels via I²C/GPIO — supporting temperature, humidity, vibration, CO₂, PM2.5, PIR, and more — from a single BLE IC.
C
Edge AI Processing
EDGE AI
Deploys on-device classification and threshold-based inference, enabling real-time anomaly detection with zero cloud dependency for instant response.
T
Ultra-Low Power BLE
BLE TRANSMISSION
BLE technology delivers years of battery life from a single coin cell. Supports long-range mode (>300m) and mesh networking for dense deployments.

ARCHITECTURE

Product Architecture Overview

BLE Sensor Hub
SH-100 / SH-200
  • Multi-Channel I²C sensor interface (2–8 channels)
  • Edge AI on-device classification (Normal / Warning / Critical)
  • Data Logging — >30 days offline storage
  • BLE Long Range (>300m) & Mesh networking
  • Programmable thresholds for each channel
  • Coin-cell or external power options
SmartBeacon
HB100 / HB120
  • BLE advertising tag — no pairing required
  • Pre-programmed firmware — zero code
  • Built-in temperature sensor (HB120, ±1°C)
  • Open/close detection via magnetic or capacitive sensing
  • Data Logging for temperature history (HB120)
  • Ultra-thin form factor (<0.8mm for label integration)
🔋 Coin Cell Battery — All products are designed for years of battery life from standard coin cell batteries (CR2032, CR1220). No recharging, no wiring.
